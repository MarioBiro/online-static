Object in Dark (2021)

19 days ago

Mixed Media Painting on A3 Paper

Perception of time changes rapidly in drastic enviroments. The one we signed for was only hope for lot of us so we got on board. Long journey was ahead of us and time went slower, longer we were in. Darkness started to spread and only way to remain conscious was to embrace the dark side and find the beauty hidden inside. Longer the journey, slower the time embrace the dark to stay in your mind.

“We can easily forgive a child who is afraid of the dark; the real tragedy of life is when men are afraid of the light.” ― Plato

Bio

Born in mid 90' in rhytm of cultural changes. Always fascinated by all artforms around, but it was at later stage of life when appreciation for abstraction was born. Idea of non calculated creation evolved into destructive expression of emotion via color and it's surroundings. What many consider damaged is not always non-functional. But joy of creation involves extraction of anger and human will feel whatever love or hate is the right emotion for the exact moment.
