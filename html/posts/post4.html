Study XX (2021)

65 days ago

Mixed Media Painting on A3 Paper

It is exactly 20 years since this ship left the ground. Floating in the space for this much time is never an easy task. Not all of us are well manered travelers tho, bottom floors are often occupied by space junkies with lack of understanding for sleep importance. Raving for days in dark is what they live for and on this journey there is no one to stop them. Noise pollution they create coule easily throw one off the ballance back on the ground but not here. Well hidden in storage areas but you can track the marks they produce all over the place. Meaning is hidden for the first time but once you follow the path more answers are being questioned.

“It takes a tribe to raise a human.” ― Yuval Noah Harari, Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind
