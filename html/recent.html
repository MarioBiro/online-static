Vandalé Deconstruct (2021)

4 days ago

Mixed Media Painting on A3 Paper

Dark night, different planet. Passion for urbanistic expression still same. While it was sleepy night for most of the species, some individuals decided to stay awake and cause the harm. General public might feel threaten by these living forms but one thing thay have all in common is ignorance to all opinions. What remains on the plate is purely emotion driven painting which consists of various destructive elements.

“Vandals listen only when others are stronger. If vandals are equal or stronger Their word is the last word.” ― Dejan Stojanovic

Bio

Born in mid 90' in rhytm of cultural changes. Always fascinated by all artforms around, but it was at later stage of life when appreciation for abstraction was born. Idea of non calculated creation evolved into destructive expression of emotion via color and it's surroundings. What many consider damaged is not always non-functional. But joy of creation involves extraction of anger and human will feel whatever love or hate is the right emotion for the exact moment.
